of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2022. I received the master’s degree in communication engineering from the Chinese Unifersity of Hongkong, Shenzhen, China, in 2024. I am very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Man-On Pun from the Chinese University of Hongkong (Shenzhen) and Prof. Cuiyun Gao from Harbin Institude of Technology (Shenzhen). My research interest includes computer vision (CV), natural language processing (NLP), and large language models (LLMs).
